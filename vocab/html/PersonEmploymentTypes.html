dc:description
  • (en) In CERIF terms, administrator is a role in e.g. the person-organisation relationship; that is, the classification term (cfTerm) "Administrator" identified by its cfClassificationIdentifier (a uuid) and reused in link entities (CERIF Task Group). An administrator directly employed by an organisation (RMAS Project).
skos:definition
  • (en) An administrator directly employed on an activity or a person not directly involved in the activity but with information related to the activity (e.g. finance, computing, hr), where they are not a contact.
