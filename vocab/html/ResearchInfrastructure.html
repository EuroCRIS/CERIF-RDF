  • (en) In CERIF, the concept of research infrastructure is defined as a term in the vocabulary. A research infrastructure in CERIF terms subsumes the three entities service cfService (cfSrv), equipment cfEquipment (cfEquip) and facility cfFacility (cfFacil); these are logic and physic enitites in the CERIF data model.
skos:definition
  • (en) A European Research Infrastructure is a facility or (virtual) platform that provides the scientific community with resources and services to conduct top-level research in their respective fields. These research infrastructures can be single-sited or distributed or an e-infrastructure, and can be part of a national or international network of facilities, or of interconnected scientific instrument networks. The infrastructure should: offer top quality scientific and technological performance, that should be recognised as being of European relevance offer access to scientific users from Europe and beyond through a transparent selection process on the basis of excellence have stable and effective management
